Payroll Management System Details

The Payroll Management System helps organizations efficiently calculate employee salaries, deductions, and net pay. It ensures accuracy and saves time with its user-friendly design.

Key Features:

  • Employee Information Management
  • Salary Calculation with Tax and Benefits
  • Automated Payslip Generation
  • Secure Login for Admin Access

Example Forms

Employee Information Form: Used to store employee details, such as ID, name, Address, position, and department.

Salary Computation Form: Calculates gross salary, deductions, net pay and print payslip.
